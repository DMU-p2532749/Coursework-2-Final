The overall concept for this website is to create a place where anyone can sell/auction their clothes and other users can buy them; the site itself will also purchase clothes on drops and sell them officially at the current re-sale price. Any sales from the site itself will be 100% legit and online receipts will be available, the clothing auctioned by the users themselves will not be tested to see if they are legitimate, however they can send their clothing to the website to be tested by us and then be put on the store as usual but with a marking on the sale stating it has been tested and is official.

To reach out for any additional questions about the functionality of the site or the testing process contact us though our email, 'Garms4YouInfo@gmail.com'
